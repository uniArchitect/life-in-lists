Designing a Building 101

These are the 5 basic steps to designing a building

  • Programming
  • Schematic Design
  • Design Development
  • Construction Documents
  • Construction

  1. Programming: Start with taking into account the client's needs and formulize a plan to block out spaces and set up general concepts for the building form and material.
  2. Schematic Design:Take your research and develop basic drawings and ideas of materials and finishes with consultants.
  3. Design Development: Push your design one step further into detail and get your building permits and consultants' work finalized.
  4. Construction Documents: Design the details and create a drawing set you can build from with a builder.
  5. Construction: Bid your drawings and oversee the construction of the project.
