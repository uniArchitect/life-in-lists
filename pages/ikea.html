IKEA Wishlist

There are a many items at IKEA to help build yourself a budget friendly, but aesthetically pleasing home! Follow these steps and you'll have an Instagram worthy living space in no time!

  • Candleholders
  • Unscented Candles
  • Placemats
  • Flat Plates
  • Cloth Napkins
  • Faux Flowers
  • Table Runner
  • Wine Glasses
  • Water Goblets
  1. Set up the Table Runner along the table length-wise
  2. Lay Placemats across chair positions
  3. Set up Cloth Napkins and Wine Glasses or Water Goblets at each placemat
  4. Flat Plates are next to be placed
  5. Finally, lay out the Candleholders and Unscented Candles with the Faux Flowers in a nice vase

Enjoy the new addition to your home!
